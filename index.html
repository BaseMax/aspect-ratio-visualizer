About Aspect Ratios

Common Ratios

  • 16:9 - Standard HD video and modern displays
  • 4:3 - Classic TV and computer monitors
  • 21:9 - Ultra-wide cinema and gaming displays
  • 1:1 - Square format (Instagram posts)
  • 16:10 - Common for laptops and tablets

A-Series Paper

  • A4 - 210mm × 297mm (Most common document size)
  • A3 - 297mm × 420mm (Posters, diagrams)
  • A5 - 148mm × 210mm (Notebooks, flyers)
  • Ratio - √2:1 (1.414:1) for all A-series
